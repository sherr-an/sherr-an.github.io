About Me

Hi there, my name is Xinyue (Sherry) An, and I am a first-year Neuroscience PhD student at Northwestern Univeristy. I have always been interested in unknown, mysterior things, especially the brain that sits on top of our body as the core of intelligence. Starting my undergrad as a Biology major, I later shifted gear toward Neuroscience and found my passion in the elegant fusion between Neuroscience and Computer Science - the studies of the two potent intelligent systems. Because one shall only use a diamond to cut another diamond, to me, artificial intelligence could be the best tool to interpret natural intelligence.

Outside of lab, I like to make espresso drinks and cocktails - one to sober, the other to numb. Not a master yet but enough to make myself happy. I also enjoy music, podcast, reading, and hiking.
